Round 1:WordPuzzle Instructions

  • You have to be registered, using your Email-ID to play the game.
  • You will be given a 20 X 20 matrix.
  • Words are arranged vertically,horizontally,diagonally and also in reverse order.
  • You have to find the words in matrix which have been mentioned in box beside it.
  • You have to click on first letter of word then drag, until the last letter to select it.
  • You can also drag in reverse order(from last letter to first) to select it.
  • Once a word is found then it gets strike-through in the box.
  • There will be 15 minutes. Timer will start once you click start game.
  • Find all words as soon as possible.
  • Assessment will be based on time and no. of words found.
  • Important :Guess word in puzzle have 5 bonus points if found.
  • You must mark it on puzzle before the last-word and before timer runs out,for bonus points.

Round 2:CodeRelay Instructions

  • You will be given 5 problem statements to solve.
  • Each new problem statement will be given on solving the previous problem statement.

  • Participant who can solve this question do not need to solve the others.
  • If anyone solves the goldenegg question in given time limit, then he /she will be directly declared as Winner.
  • If no-one choose to solve the Golden Egg question,then the participant who solves most of the questions
    among the given 5 problem statement will be declared as Winner.
